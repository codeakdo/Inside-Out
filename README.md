# Inside Out ✨

### *Your brain called. It wants this app.*

Let’s be real: our minds are messy. One minute you’re a ray of golden sunshine, and the next you’re a puddle of blue reflection. **Inside Out** is the cozy digital home for all those feelings. No judgment, no "streaks" to stress about—just you, your thoughts, and some really cool-looking orbs.

---

##  What’s the Vibe?

### 🔮 Meet the Mood Orbs
Forget boring spreadsheets. We turn your feelings into **Mood Orbs**. Logged a mix of Joy, Anxiety, and Gratitude? Watch them swirl together into a custom gradient that’s as unique (and complicated) as you are. 

### ✍️ Brain Dumps (The "Thought Composer")
Got a million tabs open in your head? Close them here. Whether it’s a deep realization or just what you had for lunch, the Thought Composer is your private, judgment-free zone to let it all out.

### 📈 "How am I actually doing?"
Our **Insights** tab isn't just numbers; it’s a mirror. See your emotional trends over time and realize that, hey, you’re actually doing a great job at this whole "human" thing.

### 🏠 Your Feelings, on your Home Screen
Our **Widgets** are basically a mood ring for your iPhone. Get a quick glimpse of your dominant emotion or your latest thought without even opening the app.

---

## 🛠 What’s Under the Hood?

We built this with the "good stuff" so it’s fast, smooth, and won’t make your phone sweat:

*   **Swift & SwiftUI:** Because declarative code is just... *chef’s kiss*.
*   **MVVM Architecture:** Keeping the logic clean and the views pretty.
*   **WidgetKit:** Making your home screen 100% more soulful.
*   **Privacy First:** Your data stays on your device. Your secrets are safe with us (and your backup).

## 🚀 Get It Running (The "Developer Dance")

1.  **Grab the code:**
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/yourusername/inside-out.git
    ```
2.  **Xcode Time:**
    Pop open `Inside Out.xcodeproj`. 
3.  **The Magic Button:**
    Hit `Cmd + R`, pick a simulator (the iPhone 15 Pro looks great, just saying), and let the orbs begin.
